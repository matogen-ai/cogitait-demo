Telecoms

Telecoms

Risk Assessment

COGITAIT leverages advanced data engineering, modelling, and alternative data to enhance risk assessment within the telecom industry. Our solutions improve credit risk profiling for customers with established credit histories and enable accurate credit scoring for those with thin or no credit history. Driving innovation in emerging markets, particularly in regions lacking robust credit registries. Success is easy to see in the millions of credit applications our solution processes every month.

Churn Reduction

Customer churn is a significant concern in the telecommunications industry directly impacting profitability. We leveraged a telecoms customer data to manage voluntary churn and reactivate dormant customer accounts. Using techniques such as weights of evidence, logistic regression, and random forests, we identified customers likely to churn. Alerts were then triggered for these accounts, which were sent to a retention queue for proactive retention efforts.

Data Analytics

In the telecoms market, we harness disruptive technologies to provide flexible, faster services that reach underserved segments. By utilizing alternative data sources, such as mobile phone usage, we create innovative scoring solutions and help drive targeted campaigns to retain and grow the subscriber base. This same technology is used to manage cannibalisation of "own market" by new product launches.
